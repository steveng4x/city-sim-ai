## 2024-05-22 - Missing Interactive Labels
**Learning:** Simulation controls often rely solely on iconography (Play, Pause, Skip), which is inaccessible to screen reader users and unclear for some visual users.
**Action:** Always add `aria-label` and `title` attributes to icon-only buttons to provide context for both assistive technology and hover states.
